iPhone 16

Specifications

  • Display: 6.7-inch Super Retina XDR Display
  • Processor: A18 Bionic Chip
  • RAM: 6GB
  • Storage: 128GB, 256GB, 512GB, 1TB
  • Battery Life: Up to 22 hours of video playback
  • Camera: Dual 12MP (Wide and Ultra Wide)
  • Ports: Lightning Port
  • Weight: 8.03 ounces

Usage Guidelines

  • Turn on the iPhone by pressing and holding the side button until the Apple logo appears.
  • To set up the iPhone, follow the on-screen instructions for Apple ID, Face ID, and Wi-Fi setup.
  • For connecting external accessories, use the Lightning port or Bluetooth for wireless devices.
  • To conserve battery, enable Low Power Mode under "Settings" when the battery reaches 20% or less.
  • Use the "Settings" app to adjust sound, display, and other preferences for your iPhone.
  • For troubleshooting, visit Apple Support or use the "Diagnostics" app for issue detection.
